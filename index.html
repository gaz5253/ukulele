Ukulele types!

Soprano

Soprano – the smallest ukulele, about 21 inches long, with a bright and cheerful sound

Concert

Concert is slightly larger than the soprano – around 23 inches, louder and more comfortable for adults to play.

Tenor

Tenor measures about 26 inches, offering a deeper and richer tone.

Bariton

Baritone is the largest ukulele, around 30 inches long, with a warm sound similar to a guitar.
